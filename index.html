Mission Journey Goal Purpose Aim Quest

CompLIT is designed to combine two otherwise opposite aspects, tech and literature, to work together. Most of society is leaning towards a more tech-oriented life and further from the classical art of enjoying literature. Through CompLIT, we want to be able to redirect and provide the opportunity of using tech to connect with others in the journey of rediscovering the love of literature.
